Getting There

The closest airports are Savannah/Hilton Head International airport and Hilton Head domestic airport. We would recommend Savannah/Hilton Head International as it generally has better rates, more direct flights and is not significantly farther from the venue.

We would also highly recommend renting a car for the weekend. While there are ride-sharing services on the island (Lyft etc), to enjoy the great restaurants, beaches and golf courses a car is recommended.
